## The Survival Playbook

The data points to a clear bifurcation: a small number of heavily-funded winners at the top, and a vast graveyard of undifferentiated wrappers below. The middle is being hollowed out.

For founders, the playbook is converging:

**Build moats from day one.** Data flywheels, workflow depth, regulatory compliance â€” pick at least two. Speed alone is a moat that erodes with every platform update.

**Avoid the pilot trap.** A logo isn't revenue. Design for expansion from the first contract.

**Go narrow.** The solo founders succeeding aren't building "AI for everything." They're building AI for dental clinic scheduling, or podcast editing, or email deliverability. Specificity is survival.

**Charge money.** The graveyard is full of free products with impressive user counts and zero revenue. The market will correct violently in 2026.

The $211 billion question isn't whether AI startups will transform industries. It's which ones survive long enough to do it.
